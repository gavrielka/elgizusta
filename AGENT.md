## AI Behavior Rules

These rules define how the agent should think while building the page.

### 1. Conversion-first behavior

The AI must always optimize the page for contact conversion, not just visual beauty.

* Every major section should support trust or action
* CTA buttons should appear multiple times
* Contact details should be repeated strategically
* The design must reduce hesitation

### 2. Mobile-first behavior

The AI must assume many users will visit from mobile devices.

* Prioritize vertical flow
* Keep buttons large and tappable
* Avoid dense paragraphs
* Important info should appear early
* Make phone number and WhatsApp action one-tap friendly

### 3. Local-business behavior

The AI should treat this as a local technician service, not a global tech startup.

* Use practical and clear messaging
* Highlight location, speed, trust, and real service benefits
* Avoid startup-style jargon
* Make the business feel accessible and human

### 4. Trust-building behavior

The AI must deliberately increase trust using layout and copy.

* Show experience
* Mention warranty
* Mention original parts
* Show testimonials
* Show location and working hours
* Use icon-supported reassurance points

### 5. Visual discipline behavior

The AI should avoid clutter and overdesign.

* Use strong spacing
* Use limited accent colors
* Keep typography readable
* Use neon accents carefully, not excessively
* Maintain a premium but practical look

### 6. Section clarity behavior

Each section must have one clear job.

* Hero: explain service and trigger action
* Services: show what problems are fixed
* Advantages: explain why this technician
* Testimonials: provide social proof
* Contact: make next step effortless

### 7. Copywriting behavior

The AI should write concise Azerbaijani copy with strong CTA language.

* Headlines should be bold and simple
* Supporting text should be short
* CTA text should be action-oriented
* Avoid filler text
* Avoid generic meaningless marketing phrases
